Hng Internship

August 15, 2021 2-minute read
internship
hng • internship

I recently signed up for HNGi8 internship organized by zuri team. It is a fast-paced training program for people interested in making a career in tech where participants are given tasks to complete based on your chosen track and upon successful completion, advance to next stages. In this article, I will be documenting my goal for joining this internship and what I want to achieve.

The internship has 10 stages and lasts for about 2-3 months and as of now, I am currently in the first stage. My goal is to reach the final stage and win a t-shirt 😄 and more importantly to learn from mentors and make friends with like minds, to build my network as I progress in this early stage of my career as a software developer. I signed up for the backend track, and I intend to learn and use the Go programming language.

As a requirement for advancing to the next stage, we have been given a task to write this article, sharing links to introductory tutorial on various topics.
